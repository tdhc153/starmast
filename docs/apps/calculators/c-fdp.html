Calculator: Fractions, decimals, percentages

Author

Tom Coleman and Millie Harris

Summary
A calculator to convert between fractions, decimals, and percentages.
Fractions • Decimals • Percentages Converter

Fractions • Decimals • Percentages Converter

Enter a value in one form and convert to the other two.
Supports mixed numbers (for example: 1 3/4) and recurring decimals using parentheses (for example: 0.(3), 1.2(45), 33.(3)).

Version history
